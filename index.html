Jeanette O'Brien

Software Developer | Graphic Designer | Illustrator

I am Software developer, Graphic Designer and Digital Illustrator residing in Raleigh, North Carolina with my husband, Aaron; two cats, Jango and Oxford; and dog, Garvey. I value clean code and out-of-the-box thinking about design and software developent.

Technoloy and art have always been a huge part of my life. Some of my fondest memories are of learning to draw my favorite cartoon characters and building computer towers with my father. As I grew older, I leveraged my knowledge of and passion for technology to develop my design, illustrative and software development skills.

I enjoy the challenge offered by every creative opportunity. My goal is deliver effective technical and visual solutions with professionalism, enthusiasm and dedication.
